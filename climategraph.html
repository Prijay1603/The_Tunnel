Climate Graph.





A climate graph is a graph that displays the monthly average temperature as well as the monthly average rainfall. It is basically a bar graph and a line graph put together. It consists of a title, an X axis, and two Y axes. Along the x axis, there are the months from a year. These are from January all the way to December on the same year. Along the primary Y axis, there is the amount of rainfall in millimetres. Along the secondary Y axis, there is the temperature in degrees Celsius. The bar graph is used to display the average temperature against the Secondary Y axis. The line graph shows the average rainfall against the Primary Y axis. In the story, the climate graph displays the average temperature and average rainfall for New Delhi.
