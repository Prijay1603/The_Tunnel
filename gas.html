Gas



Gas is one of the three main states of matter, the other two are solids and liquids. There is another state that is plasma. Plasma is literally charged gas. Gas has two options for a physical and chemical change. One of them is cooling and the other one is deposition. Cooling is when the gas changes into a liquid. Deposition only occurs in extremely cold climates. It is a process where the gas cools directly into a solid without first becoming a liquid like in cooling. In the story, the gas refers to the state of matter the water is in.
