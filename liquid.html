Liquid.



Liquid is one of the three main states of matter, the other two are solids, and gasses. Liquids have two options for a physical and chemical change. One of them is freezing and the other one is evaporating. Freezing is when the liquid changes into a solid.. Evaporation is when the liquid becomes a gas. In the story, the liquid refers to the rain that falls from the clouds.
