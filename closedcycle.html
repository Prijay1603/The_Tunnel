Closed Cycle.



A closed cycle is a cycle that cannot be naturally stopped. One example of this is the water cycle. In the water cycle, water is constantly being evaporated, condensated, and precipitated. However, none of the water is lost during this process so it is the same water that has been circulating for the last 3.8 billion years. In the story, the closed cycle is referring to the water cycle.
