Solid



Solids are one of the three main states of matter, the other two are gasses and liquids. Most solids has two options for a physical and chemical change. One of them is melting and the other one is sublimation. Melting is when the solid changes into a liquid. Sublimation only occurs in extremely hot climates. It is a process where the solid heats directly into a gas without first becoming a liquid like in melting. In the story, the solid refers to snow, hail, or sleet that can fall from the clouds.
