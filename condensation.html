Condensation



Condensation is the process of the water vapor uniting to form clouds. This occurs due to the water vapor reaching the troposphere and cooling down. When the anything cools down, it contracts to preserve heat. This is the same as with clouds, water droplets contract and joins with more water droplets to create clouds. When the water droplets become extremely heavy, they precipitate.In the story, the clouds have already been through condensation.
