Percipitation



Precipitation plays a key role in the water cycle. It happens after condensation and before evaporation. This is the step where the water droplets become too heavy to be carried in the clouds and so they fall to the surface of the Earth. The water droplets can descend in a variety of ways, some include rain, hail, snow or sleet. In the story, the clouds are yet to rain. It is not specified in which form it will rain.
